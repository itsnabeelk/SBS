PURPOSE, MISSION, VISION, AND VALUES

We SBS, Our mission is to create long-term value in Kingdom of Saudi Arabiya, Gulf Countries and globally by strategically managing a diverse portfolio of businesses. We are committed to fostering innovation, operational excellence, and sustainable growth within our subsidiaries. Through responsible leadership and prudent financial management, we aim to maximize the potential of each subsidiary while contributing positively to the communities in which we operate. Our vision is to be a trusted partner in driving success, profitability, and positive societal impact across our entire business ecosystem.

We SBS, our vision is to be a regionally and globally recognized leader in the different types of business sectors. Our vision is to be the world's most trusted and respected holding company, known for our commitment to long-term value creation through a diverse portfolio of businesses. In this respect our commitment is to build and maintain quality of technical standards in all Fields of infrastructure industries, electric power Factory and Communications industry , We envision infrastructure that enhances the quality of life for people of regional Gulf Countries and globally, fostering economic growth, connectivity and access to essential services to invent the next industrial era, to cure the world and to lead the information revolution by creating innovative, technology-driven solutions that benefit humanity.
